Bangladesh International School & College (BISC) is an English Medium institution managed by Bangladesh Army Headquarters, Dhaka Cantonment. BISC started its journey on 14th March 1995. The college today is one of the most prestigious institutions in the country with around 2500 students from Nursery to A level & HSC.
